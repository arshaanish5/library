Ram c/o Anandhi

About the book The story is about Ram, a dreamer from Alleppey, Kerala who moves to Chennai to study filmmaking and writing. His life changes when he meets Anandhi, a receptionist at his college, and their relationship evolves from animosity to passion. The book explores themes of love, loss, friendship, hope, and resilience.Some say the book is cinematic and visually engaging, with an easy-to-understand writing style.
Author : Akhil P. Dharmajan, the author of Ram C/O Anandhi, lived in Chennai for a couple of years and learned filmmaking to gain experiences for writing the book.

Language: Malayalam
Category: Novel
