Ram c/o Anandhi

Ram C/o Anandhi is a novel by Akhil P. Dharmajan that tells the story of a young man from Kerala who moves to Chennai to study filmmaking and writing. It is an emotionally charged, unputdownable novel about love, revenge, loss and friendship that has taken readers by storm

The Sword in the Stone

The Sword in the Stone by T. H. White is a classic story about the early life of King Arthur, from his time as a boy named Wart to his becoming the rightful king of England.There was a curious boy named Wart and a kind old wizard named Merlyn. Transformed by Merlyn into the forms of his fantasy, Wart learns the value of history from a snake, of education from a badger, and of courage from a hawk--the lessons that help turn a boy into a man.

Ente Kadha

Ente Katha (My Story) is an autobiography by Kamala Das that chronicles her life from childhood to adulthood.This book is about Aami (Kamala), starting from her childhood and her village. It also depicts her teenage love towards a neighbour of the same age. Her childhood in colonised Calcutta is also explained vividly. Her failed marriage, the birth of her children and her extramarital affairs are addressed in this work.

The Invisible Man

The Invisible Man is a science fiction novel by H.G. Wells that was published in 1897.The story is about a scientist named Griffin who discovers how to make himself invisible and uses his power for evil. Griffin's invisibility leads to isolation, madness, and terrorism. The novel is an allegory about the dangers of unchecked science

Oru Police Surgeon Ormakurippukal

Oru Police Surgeonte Ormakkurippukal is a memoir by Dr. B. Umadathan, a forensic surgeon from Kerala, that details some of the most interesting and unusual cases he worked on. The book is a mix of crime story, memoir, and nonfiction, and it includes details on how forensic science was used to solve the cases.Some cases were really interesting. At times I was dumb struck reading how people committed suicide and about some homicides!

The Girl on the Train

The Girl on the Train is a novel by Paula Hawkins about a woman who becomes involved in a murder investigation after witnessing something shocking on the train.Rachel Watson is a divorced woman who takes the train to work in London, where she watches a couple she names Megan and Scott Hipwell. She creates a fantasy life for them in her head, but one day she sees something shocking and becomes involved in their disappearance.
