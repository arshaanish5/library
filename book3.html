Mercury Island

Mercury island is a mystery island. a college professor named nicolson found an old book and a map. so he is taking a break from his teaching life and going for an adventure journey to the mercury island.The story follows a college professor named Nicholson who finds a hidden book in an ancient statue he buys at an auction. The book leads him to Mercury Island, a mysterious island in the middle of the ocean where the Mayan people have been hiding for generations. Nicholson takes a break from teaching to travel to the island, where he and those who follow him encounter unexpected challenges.
Author: Akhil P Dharmajan is an author from Alappuzha.

Language: Malayalam
Category: Action and Adventure
